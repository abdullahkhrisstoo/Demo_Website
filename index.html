About FC Barcelona

FC Barcelona, commonly referred to as Barça, is a professional football club based in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. Founded in 1899 by a group of Swiss, English, German, and Catalan footballers led by Joan Gamper, the club has become a symbol of Catalan culture and Catalanism, hence the motto "Més que un club" ("More than a club").

History

FC Barcelona has a rich history in both domestic and international football. Here are some key highlights:

  • La Liga Titles: 26 (as of 2024)
  • UEFA Champions League Titles: 5
  • UEFA Europa League Titles: 5
  • Copa del Rey Titles: 31

Stadium

The home stadium of FC Barcelona is the Camp Nou, the largest stadium in Spain and Europe, with a seating capacity of over 99,000 spectators.
